# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Office employees and corporate teams who spend hours daily inside the messenger. Their primary job is fast, distraction-free communication, requiring high text legibility, easy navigation, and visual comfort during long working sessions under varying ambient light.

## Product Purpose
A fast, reliable corporate messenger application that serves as the central hub for team collaboration. Success is defined by an interface that recedes into the background to prioritize message content and team activity, ensuring low cognitive load and rapid workflow navigation.

## Brand Personality
"Minimalist with precision" (professional, clean, focus-oriented). The interface uses `#213786` (indigo) strictly as an accent to indicate status (presence, notifications, active sections) and primary actions, ensuring the rest of the workspace remains calm and uncluttered.

## Anti-references
- SaaS clichés (e.g., glassmorphism, heavy drop shadows, decorative cards, or side-stripe borders).
- Generic visual clutter (e.g., uppercase wide-tracked kickers/eyebrows on every section, redundant numbered labels like 01/02/03).
- Low-contrast text hierarchy (e.g., light gray body text on off-white).

## Design Principles
1. **Less is more (Minimalism with precision)**: Visual framework elements disappear to let content (conversations, messages, media) shine.
2. **Brand as an accent**: Indigo is reserved exclusively for primary actions and active status signals.
3. **Monospaced metadata for precision**: Message timestamps, presence indicators, and counts use `IBM Plex Mono` to convey the feel of a precise, high-performance tool.
4. **Borders over shadows**: Structural division uses 1px hair-thin lines rather than heavy shadows or complex gradients.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1 AA level compliance (ensuring >= 4.5:1 text-to-background contrast ratios for all text, including placeholders).
- Full dark and light mode support tailored for long-hour screen comfort.
- Respecting user motion settings by disabling non-essential transitions when `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` is active.
